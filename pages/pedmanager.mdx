# Sleepless Ped Manager

<Callout type="info">
    this resource is compatible with our `sleepless_interact` resource!
</Callout>

A FiveM ped manager for spawning local peds within distance of players and adding target/interact options to them

<ResourceLinks repo="https://github.com/Sleepless-Development/sleepless_pedmanager" />

## Installation

### Download the [release](https://github.com/Sleepless-Development/sleepless_pedmanager/releases)

## Usage

add to your resource folder and ensure `sleeless_pedmanager` in your server.cfg

```cfg
ensure sleepless_pedmanager
```

You can now use the pedmanager exports inside of your resources.
